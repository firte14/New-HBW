My Journey

Hi I'm Rosa and I have a real passion for creative design. I'm never happier than when I'm pushing the envelope of what can be done with HTML and CSS.

I came into web design from an art background but soon realized that I needed to develop my technical skills as well. So I did a lot of online courses that pushed what I know, then used the support and experience of my colleagues to hone my skills.

My work with Hot Beans has been very varied and I enjoy the challenge of not knowing what industry our next client will come from. It keeps me on my toes and my creativity fresh.

"The part I enjoy most about my job is being both technical and creative - it's the perfect mix."

My Day-to-Day

  • Take a lead role in maintaining and developing client's sites
  • Attend meetings with internal and external clients to plan and develop site content, style and appearance
  • Use web content management systems (CMS)
  • Analyze website usage statistics
  • Write reports for senior managers, clients and partnership organizations
  • Carry out quality assurance checks on content

My Advice for New Developers

Don't be afraid to experiment and make mistakes. Some of my best learning has come from projects that didn't go as planned. Also, build a portfolio of personal projects - they're great for learning and demonstrating your skills.
